---
title: Platform Management Center
---

:::important
The Platform Management Center is available under the ClearML Enterprise plan.
:::

The **Platform Management Center** is an administrative interface for managing a ClearML Enterprise deployment. It gives 
platform administrators a single place to manage all tenants, control shared resources, and monitor usage and costs 
across the entire deployment.

From the Platform Management Center, administrators can:
* **Manage tenants** - Create and configure tenants, set user quotas, generate admin credentials, and track per-tenant usage 
  and costs.
* **Monitor platform-wide usage and costs** - View aggregated metered usage, cost breakdowns, and cluster usage data across  
  all tenants.
* **Configure cluster storage** - Define volume templates and platform-level template variables that tenant administrators 
  and users can apply to their workloads.
* **Manage platform applications** - Deploy and manage ClearML applications across the platform, with per-tenant control.

For deployment instructions, see [Platform Management Center deployment](deploying_clearml/enterprise_deploy/extra_configs/platform_management_center_deploy.md).

For a full feature-by-feature description, see the [Platform Management Center reference](platform_management_center/pmc_overview.md).
